望月怀远

Xinyu Jian ♥ 14 September 2019 1 min

< center> 望月怀远 < /center>

张九龄

海上生明月，天涯共此時。

情人怨遙夜，竟夕起相思。

滅燭憐光滿，披衣覺露滋。

不堪盈手贈，還寢夢佳期。

Gazing at the Moon Thinking of Afar

ZHANG Jiuling

A bright moon rises over the sea, all over the world shares this moment.

Sentimental soul complains about this long night, while yearning throughout the night.

I want to feel this shiny moon and put out candlelight, after putting on a robe, I am still chilled by the dewy.

What a pity that I can not hold the moonlight in hand to give it to you, I would better go back to sleep, maybe we can gather together in the dream.

身在异乡，周遭尽是不同的肤色、面孔，第一个中秋节，难免思想之情涌动。月饼太贵，没有吃上，月亮也藏在乌云中。幸而如此，感情没了泛滥的借口。
